The comma splice occurs when a comma is used to connect two independent clauses.

Example: Jim usually gets on with everybody, he is an understanding person.

If you have two independent clauses that need to be separated, you can do so by using a full stop:

Jim usually gets on with everybody. He is an understanding person.
